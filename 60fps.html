60FPS vs 30FPS

This page exists to showcase the difference between 60FPS (frames per second) and 30FPS. Each game video is displayed twice, one for each framerate. I recommend watching the lower framerate video before the higher because I believe it tends to make the difference even more apparent.

Games:

  • Homefront
  • Dark Souls
  • Battlefield 3
  • Batman: Arkham City
  • Spec Ops: The Line
  • Hitman: Absolution

Why? Simply, to combat the notion that there is no difference between framerates >=30. I'll leave it up to you which you think is better; in my mind 60FPS is the clear winner.
